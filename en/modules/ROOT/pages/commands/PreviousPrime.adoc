PreviousPrime( <Number> )::
  Returns the greatest prime smaller than the entered number.

[EXAMPLE]
====

`++PreviousPrime(10000)++` yields _9973_.

====

[NOTE]
====

See also xref:/commands/NextPrime.adoc[NextPrime Command].

====
